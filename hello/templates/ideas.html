The 10,000 Ideas Initiative

Introducing the 10,000 Ideas Initiative on this auspicious occasion of Akshay Tritiya

What is it all about?

  • Everyone has Ideas, Dreams, a willingness to create something significant in life.
  • 10,000 Ideas is an initiative in that direction which tries to bring those ideas to life.

When has it started?

  • Date: 9th May, 2016
  • Time: 6:30am IST
  • Day: Monday
  • Occasion: Akshay Tritiya
  • Reason: Any initiative taken up on Akshay Tritiya continues to grow and brings happiness and prosperity
